The EV Game

Electric vehicles (EV) are helping drive forward the fight against climate change. As the world transitions from gas-powered to electric vehicles, environmental issues like lack of efficient recycling methods and resource-intensive material production are exacerbated. Play The EV Game to learn more. Press SPACE to avoid approaching rocks, and to collect exclamation marks. Avoiding rocks and staying on the road longer will help rack up more “green” points. Collect more exclamations and learn about future challenges in the EV industry.
